SecureShare Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

SecureShare ("we", "us") provides a secure document-sharing application. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: email address and display name you provide at sign-up.
  • Documents and images: files you upload are encrypted on your device before upload. We store only the encrypted content; we cannot read it.
  • Encryption keys: your RSA public key is stored to enable secure sharing. Your private key never leaves your device.
  • Device identifier: a derived device hash used for forensic watermarking and access binding.
  • Sharing metadata: recipient email addresses, access grants, expiry, and access/audit logs.
  • Analytics & diagnostics (optional): usage and error data, collected only if you enable the corresponding toggles in Settings.
  • Security events: screenshot/recording detections and similar events, used to protect shared documents.

2. How we use it

  • To operate document sharing, access control, and expiry/revocation.
  • To embed forensic watermarks that identify the source of a leaked document.
  • To detect and log security events on documents you own or receive.
  • To improve reliability (only with your analytics/error-reporting consent).

3. Encryption

Document content is protected with AES-256-GCM, and per-recipient keys are wrapped with RSA-2048. Content is end-to-end encrypted: our servers store ciphertext only.

4. Storage and processing

Data is stored using our backend provider (Supabase) with row-level security and encryption at rest. We do not sell your data. We share content only with the recipients you explicitly choose.

5. Retention and deletion

We retain your data until you delete it or your account. You can permanently delete your account and associated data at any time from Profile → Delete Account. Immutable audit logs may be retained as required for security and legal compliance.

6. Your choices

  • Enable/disable analytics and error reporting in Settings.
  • Access or delete your data via the in-app account deletion.
  • Contact us for any privacy request.

7. Children

SecureShare is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions or requests: support@secureshare.app.
